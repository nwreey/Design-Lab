Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026
Draft template — not legal advice. This document is a general-purpose starting point and hasn't been reviewed by a lawyer or a privacy specialist. It doesn't account for specific regimes like GDPR, UAE data protection law, or others that may apply to DMR Arabia or its users. Please have this reviewed and customized by qualified counsel before relying on it.

1. Information We Collect

When you use DesignsLab AI, we collect:

  • Account information — name, email, and company details you provide when your access is set up;
  • Project inputs — the descriptions, dimensions, brand colors, and other form data you enter to generate a design;
  • Uploaded assets — logos, reference images, or product photos you upload as part of a project;
  • Usage data — basic interaction data such as which features are used and when, to help us improve the Service;
  • Language preference — stored locally in your browser (see our Cookie Policy).

2. How We Use Information

We use this information to operate the Service — generating your design concepts, maintaining your account and project history, providing support, and improving the product over time.

3. Third-Party AI Processing

Generating a design concept requires sending your project description, dimensions, and any uploaded images to third-party AI providers (for text and image generation). Those providers process this data to return the generated concept and images. We don't control those providers' independent retention practices, so avoid uploading anything you wouldn't want processed by an external AI service.

4. Data Retention

We keep your account and project data for as long as your account is active, so you can revisit past projects. If you'd like your data deleted, contact us using the details below.

5. Your Rights

Depending on where you're located, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise any of these, reach out through our Contact page.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

The Service uses browser local storage for essential functionality like remembering your language preference and keeping you signed in. See our Cookie Policy for full detail.

7. Children's Privacy

DesignsLab AI is a business tool intended for professional use and isn't directed at children. We don't knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18.

8. International Users

Depending on where you access the Service from, your information may be processed in a different country than where you're located, including by the third-party AI providers referenced in Section 3.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through our Contact page.
