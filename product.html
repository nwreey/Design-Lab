Under the Hood

Engineered, not guessed

A rendering model left to its own judgment will happily draw a doorway through a wall that's supposed to be solid. DesignsLab AI doesn't ask it to guess — the geometry is computed first, and the render is checked against it after.

1

Spatial Planning Engine

Every zone you request is sized and positioned from your booth's real dimensions — never a fixed layout stretched or shrunk to fit.

2

Structural Shell Engine

Wall thickness, open vs. closed sides, and storage room boundaries are computed as real coordinates before any image is generated.

3

Double-Deck Architecture Rules

A second floor always gets a real, structurally supported staircase and guard rails on every exposed edge — never an afterthought, never optional.

4

Multi-Camera Documentation

Every approved design is captured from a full set of elevations and perspectives — up to 13 angles for a double-deck booth, including a dedicated staircase and top-down view.

5

Orientation Lock

Every camera angle is checked against the same locked geometry the first render used, so a wall that's supposed to be open never quietly appears closed three views later.

6

Multilingual Workspace

Work in English, Arabic, French, German, Spanish, Italian, Chinese, or Japanese — with full right-to-left layout support for Arabic.
