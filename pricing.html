Questions

Pricing FAQ

What counts as "a project"?

One project is one design — an exhibition booth, event concept, or display stand — from its first generated hero image through its full set of camera views. Refining that same design with Edit Design or generating another option for it doesn't use a second project.

Do unused project credits expire?

No. Since every tier is a one-time purchase, not a subscription, your project credits stay on your account until you use them — there's no monthly reset and nothing to lose by not using them right away.

Can I buy more projects later?

Yes. You can purchase another tier at any time from your account, and the new project credits are simply added to what you already have.

What happens when I run out of project credits?

You'll be prompted to purchase another tier before starting a new project. Any designs you've already generated remain fully accessible — you're never locked out of past work.

Is there a monthly subscription option?

Not currently — every tier is a one-time purchase. If a recurring plan would suit your team better, get in touch through the Enterprise page.

Do you offer refunds?

If a project fails to generate due to a technical issue on our side, that credit is restored automatically. For other refund requests, reach out and we'll take a look on a case-by-case basis.
