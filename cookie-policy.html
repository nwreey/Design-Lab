Last updated: July 2026
Draft template — not legal advice. This document describes, in plain language, the actual local-storage and third-party requests this site makes. It's a starting point, not a legally reviewed policy — have it checked against the cookie/consent rules that apply in your users' regions (e.g. the EU ePrivacy Directive) before relying on it.

1. Cookies vs. Local Storage

DesignsLab AI doesn't currently set traditional tracking cookies. Instead, the site uses your browser's local storage — a small amount of data saved on your device — to remember basic preferences between visits.

2. What We Store

NamePurposeType
designLabLanguageRemembers the interface language you selected, so every page loads in that language.Essential, local storage

This data stays on your device and isn't shared with third parties. Clearing your browser's site data will reset it.

3. Third-Party Requests

Pages on this site load fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). Loading a font involves your browser making a request to Google's servers, which may log standard request data (like your IP address) under Google's own privacy practices.

4. Managing Your Preferences

You can clear locally stored data at any time through your browser's settings (usually under "Site settings" or "Clear browsing data"). Doing so will reset your language preference to the default.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the site's use of storage or third-party resources changes. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through our Contact page.
